Sparkour is an open-source collection of programming recipes for Apache Spark. 
Designed as an efficient way to navigate the intricacies of the Spark ecosystem, 
Sparkour aims to be an approachable, understandable, and actionable cookbook 
for distributed data processing. You can contact the author at
sparkour@urizone.net. The Sparkour home page is located at
https://sparkour.urizone.net/ .
